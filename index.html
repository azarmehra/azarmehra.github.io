I am a PhD student in computer science at Northeastern University, where I am advised by Soheil Behnezhad.
I am broadly interested in theoretical computer science, combinatorial optimization, and randomized algorithms. My research is currently focused on the theoretical aspects of big data graph algorithms, this includes graph algorithms in the sublinear, streaming, and dynamic regimes.
Before joining Northeastern I received BSc degrees in computer science and math from Sharif University.
